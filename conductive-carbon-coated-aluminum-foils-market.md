<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Oil Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive carbon coated aluminum foils are categorized into water-based and oil-based types based on their coating mediums. Water-based conductive foils utilize a water-soluble binder, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for applications requiring low toxicity. They are often used in electronics and energy storage systems. In contrast, oil-based foils incorporate organic solvents, offering enhanced conductivity and durability. This type is typically favored in high-performance applications, such as advanced battery technologies and electrical components, where robustness is essential.</p></p>
