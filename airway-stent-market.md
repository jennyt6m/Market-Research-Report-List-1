<p><p>The airway stent market comprises primarily two types: tracheal stents and laryngeal stents. Tracheal stents are designed to maintain patency in the trachea, often used for conditions like tracheal stenosis or tumors, improving airflow and breathing. Laryngeal stents, on the other hand, focus on supporting the laryngeal airway, addressing issues such as laryngeal obstruction due to various causes. Both types play crucial roles in managing airway obstruction, enhancing patient quality of life, and facilitating easier breathing.</p></p>
