Protease breaks down proteins into amino acids, enhancing nutrient uptake and supporting muscle health. Amylase assists in starch digestion, converting carbohydrates into simple sugars for energy. Together, these enzymes improve pet digestive health, enhance nutrient absorption, and support overall well-being, leading to increased demand for enzyme-based supplements in the pet care industry.</p></p>
