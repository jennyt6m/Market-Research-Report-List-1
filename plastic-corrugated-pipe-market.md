<p><p>The plastic corrugated pipe market is divided into two main types: single wall corrugated (SWC) and double wall corrugated (DWC) pipes. SWC pipes consist of a single layered structure, offering flexibility and lightweight properties, making them suitable for drainage and agricultural applications. In contrast, DWC pipes feature an extra layer, providing enhanced strength and resistance to external pressures, making them ideal for sewage, stormwater management, and underground installations. Both types cater to diverse industrial and infrastructural needs.</p></p>
