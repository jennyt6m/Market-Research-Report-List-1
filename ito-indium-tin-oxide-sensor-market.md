<p><p>The ITO sensor market is primarily categorized into film-based and glass-based sensors. Film-based ITO sensors are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for applications like touchscreens and wearable devices. They offer high transparency and conductivity while being amenable to mass production. In contrast, glass-based ITO sensors are more rigid and provide superior durability and optical clarity, making them suitable for larger displays and automotive applications. Both types leverage the conductive properties of indium tin oxide for various electronic applications.</p></p>
