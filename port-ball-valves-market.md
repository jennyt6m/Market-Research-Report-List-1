<p><p>The Port Ball Valves market consists of two primary types: Soft Seal Valves and Hard Seal Valves. Soft Seal Valves utilize elastomeric seals to provide a tight closure, suitable for low to medium pressure applications, offering excellent leak-proof performance. In contrast, Hard Seal Valves employ metal-to-metal sealing surfaces, making them ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature environments, providing durability and longevity. Both types serve critical roles in fluid control across various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
