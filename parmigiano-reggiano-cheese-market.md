<p><p>The Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese market is divided into two main types: Natural and Processed. Natural Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, renowned for its rich flavor and granular texture, is traditionally produced from unpasteurized cow's milk and aged for a minimum of 12 months, appealing to gourmet consumers. In contrast, Processed Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese undergoes alterations, often including additives or blending with other ingredients, to enhance shelf life and affordability, catering to a broader market segment seeking convenience and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
