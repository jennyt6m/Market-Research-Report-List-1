<p><ul><li>Monopolar Instruments</li><li>Bipolar Instruments</li><li>Ultrasonic Instruments</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical energy instruments encompass various types, including monopolar, bipolar, ultrasonic, and others. Monopolar instruments use a single active electrode to cut and coagulate tissue by passing current through the patient's body. Bipolar instruments utilize two electrodes to deliver energy directly to the tissue, minimizing surrounding damage. Ultrasonic instruments generate high-frequency vibrations to dissect tissue and simultaneously coagulate, offering precision and reduced thermal injury. Other instruments may include laser and radiofrequency devices, each designed for specific surgical applications and patient needs.</p></p>
