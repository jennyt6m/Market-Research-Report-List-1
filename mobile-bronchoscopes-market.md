<p><p>The mobile bronchoscope market includes two primary types: rigid and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are tubular instruments that provide excellent visualization and are typically used for larger airway procedures. They offer stability but can be less comfortable for patients. On the other hand, flexible bronchoscopes are more versatile, allowing for navigation through the airway's curvature, making them ideal for various diagnostic and therapeutic procedures. Both types play crucial roles in respiratory diagnostics and interventions, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
