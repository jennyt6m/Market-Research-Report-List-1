<p><ul><li>Solvent Metal Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Aqueous Metal Cleaning Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial metal cleaning equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: solvent metal cleaning equipment and aqueous metal cleaning equipment. Solvent metal cleaning equipment employs organic solvents to remove contaminants like oils and greases from metal surfaces, offering efficient cleaning but posing environmental and health concerns. In contrast, aqueous metal cleaning equipment uses water-based solutions, often with detergents, to achieve a more eco-friendly cleaning process, making it suitable for industries seeking sustainable practices while effectively maintaining metal cleanliness.</p></p>
