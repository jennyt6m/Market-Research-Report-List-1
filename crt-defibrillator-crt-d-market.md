<p><ul><li>Internal Defibrillator</li><li>External Defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CRT Defibrillator (CRT-D) market is divided into internal and external defibrillator segments. Internal defibrillators, implanted within the body, are designed to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when necessary, providing continuous support for patients with heart failure and arrhythmias. In contrast, external defibrillators, used outside the body, offer emergency resuscitation during cardiac events and are commonly found in public spaces and medical facilities. Both types play crucial roles in managing cardiac conditions, enhancing patient outcomes and safety.</p></p>
