<p><ul><li>Adiabatic</li><li>Direct Injection Steam</li><li>Heated Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial and industrial humidifiers market includes three main types: adiabatic, direct injection steam, and heated tank humidifiers. Adiabatic humidifiers utilize water evaporation to add moisture to the air, making them energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. Direct injection steam humidifiers inject steam directly into the air, providing precise humidity control and rapid response. Heated tank humidifiers heat water to produce steam and disperse it, offering reliable performance in larger spaces. Each type serves various industrial and commercial applications, depending on specific humidity requirements.</p></p>
