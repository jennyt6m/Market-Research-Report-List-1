<p><p>The self bag drop systems market is categorized into two main types: wall-mounted and freestanding. Wall-mounted systems are integrated into existing terminal infrastructures, maximizing space efficiency and providing a streamlined experience for passengers. Freestanding systems, on the other hand, are independent units that can be placed in various strategic locations within an airport, offering flexibility and easy access. Both types enhance the check-in process by allowing travelers to independently drop off their luggage, thus improving operational efficiency and passenger convenience.</p></p>
