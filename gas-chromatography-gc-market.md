<p><p>Gas Chromatography (GC) is an analytical technique used to separate and analyze volatile compounds in various samples, including gases, liquids, and solids. GC is widely utilized in numerous fields such as pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, food safety, and petrochemicals.</p><p>The Gas Chromatography market is experiencing significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period.
