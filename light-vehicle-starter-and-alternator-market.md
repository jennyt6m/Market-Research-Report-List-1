<p><p>The light vehicle starter and alternator market comprises two primary components: alternators and starter motors. Alternators generate electrical energy, charging the vehicle’s battery and powering electrical systems while the engine runs. Starter motors, on the other hand, provide the initial torque needed to crank the engine, enabling ignition. These components are crucial in modern vehicles, ensuring reliable performance and efficiency by converting mechanical energy into electrical energy and vice versa, thus facilitating the vehicle's functionality and longevity.</p></p>
