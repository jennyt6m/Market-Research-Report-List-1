<p><strong>The UV-LED Light Source Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Irradiation</li><li>Spot Irradiation</li><li>Surface Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-LED light source market is categorized into three main types: linear irradiation, spot irradiation, and surface irradiation. Linear irradiation provides even exposure along a continuous line, making it ideal for applications like printing and coating. Spot irradiation focuses intense UV light on a specific point, suitable for precise curing and bonding tasks. Surface irradiation emits UV light across a broad area, effectively treating larger surfaces such as in disinfection or surface sterilization applications. Each type serves distinct application needs in various industries.</p></p>
