<p><ul><li>CPI (Corrugated Plate Interceptor)</li><li>TPI (Tilted Plate Interceptor)</li><li>PPI (Parallel Plate Interceptor)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plates Interceptor market is categorized into three main types: Corrugated Plate Interceptor (CPI), Tilted Plate Interceptor (TPI), and Parallel Plate Interceptor (PPI). CPI utilizes corrugated plates to enhance sedimentation efficiency, TPI features inclined plates that facilitate faster settling of particles, while PPI employs parallel plates for effective separation of solids and liquids. Each design serves specific applications in water treatment and industrial processes, offering varying benefits in efficiency, space-saving, and overall performance in contaminant removal.</p></p>
