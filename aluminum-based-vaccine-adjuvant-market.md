<p><p>The aluminum-based vaccine adjuvant market is segmented into three primary types: aluminum potassium sulfate, aluminum phosphate, and aluminum hydroxide. Aluminum potassium sulfate is known for enhancing vaccine efficacy by improving antigen presentation. Aluminum phosphate acts similarly but offers a different solubility profile, impacting its release kinetics. Aluminum hydroxide is the most widely used adjuvant, providing a robust immune response by forming a depot effect at the injection site, prolonging antigen exposure to the immune system.</p></p>
