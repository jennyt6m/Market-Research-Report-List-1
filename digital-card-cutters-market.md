<p><p>The digital card cutters market comprises various cutting technologies, primarily blade cutting and laser cutting. Blade cutting utilizes precise mechanical blades to shear materials, making it suitable for high-speed production and thicker substrates. In contrast, laser cutting employs focused light beams to achieve intricate designs with minimal material wastage, ideal for detailed graphics and delicate materials. Each technology offers unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in industries such as printing, packaging, and crafting, facilitating efficiency and precision in card production.</p></p>
