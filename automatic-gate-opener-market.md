<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic gate opener market is primarily divided into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical openers utilize gears, chains, or motors to operate gates and are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, hydraulic openers use fluid power to move gates, offering greater strength and smooth operation, particularly for heavier gates. Each type caters to different user needs, with mechanical systems typically suitable for lighter gates and hydraulic systems preferred for larger or commercial setups due to their robustness.</p></p>
