<p><ul><li>Intra-Oral Imaging Systems</li><li>Extra-Oral Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental imaging systems market is primarily divided into two types: intra-oral and extra-oral imaging systems. Intra-oral imaging systems include devices like digital sensors and intra-oral cameras, which capture detailed images of the teeth and surrounding tissues directly within the mouth. Extra-oral imaging systems encompass panoramic x-rays and cone beam computed tomography (CBCT), which provide broader views of the dental structure and surrounding anatomy. Both types are essential for diagnostics, treatment planning, and enhancing patient care in dentistry.</p></p>
